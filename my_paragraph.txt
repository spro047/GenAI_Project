*Game of Thrones* follows a complex web of noble families in the fictional continents of Westeros and Essos, all competing for power and control of the Iron Throne. The story intertwines political intrigue, betrayals, alliances, and wars, primarily focusing on houses like Stark, Lannister, and Targaryen. As the struggle for dominance unfolds, an ancient supernatural threat—the White Walkers—emerges from the North, posing a danger to all of humanity. Key arcs include the fall and rise of major houses, Daenerys Targaryen’s quest to reclaim the throne, and Jon Snow’s role in confronting the northern threat, culminating in a final conflict that determines the fate of the realm.
